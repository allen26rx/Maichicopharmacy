Our Story

Built on Faith,
Driven by Care

MAICHICO Pharmacy Ltd. was founded by Mr. Chigozie C. Chime — an entrepreneur with a singular vision: to bring dependable, affordable pharmaceutical care to the heart of Sokoto State. What began as a dedication to community health has grown into one of the region's most trusted pharmacy destinations.

Nestled in the vibrant Hajiya Halima Estate, directly opposite the Main Market Gate, our location is more than an address — it is a landmark of accessibility. Day after day, residents, traders, and healthcare workers walk through our doors knowing they will find what they need, served with dignity and expertise.

Our shelves carry over a thousand carefully selected pharmaceutical products — from everyday essentials and chronic disease medications to vitamins, surgical supplies, and wholesale bulk orders for clinics and hospitals across the region.
